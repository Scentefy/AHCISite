Analysis

The experiment consisted of 12 students. Each participant was tested in the one of two ways; taking notes down on paper or taking notes down on a keyboard before taking a quiz. The experiment was successfully completed over all students. Data was extracted from the experiment based on the answers provided by the students. The data will serve as important information that will be analysed, providing evidence on whether the use of personal computers improve their ability to retain information.

Preference in Note Taking
